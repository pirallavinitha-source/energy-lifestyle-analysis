# 📊 Energy Level vs Lifestyle Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes how daily habits like sleep, water intake, screen time, and exercise affect energy levels.

## 📊 Dataset
- Date: March 1 – March 15, 2026
- Features:
  - Energy Level
  - Sleep Hours
  - Water Intake
  - Screen Time
  - Exercise

## 📈 Graphs
- Energy vs Date
- Sleep vs Energy
- Water vs Energy
- Screen vs Energy
- Exercise vs Energy

## 🧠 Insights
- More sleep increases energy
- More water improves energy
- Screen time reduces energy
- Exercise boosts energy

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Excel
